UROP: Boston Parking Data

MIT Geonumerics Group UROP

Worked on team of 3 undergraduate students to create interactive web interface to display parking meter data locations and data for the city of Boston

User inputs any time and day, and can specify street, and map will zoom to street if specified and show free-to-park parking meters at the given time.

Utilized JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Google Map APIs

Presented and demoed site at a final symposium to members of MIT Course 1 Community

Accelerated Aging of Calcium Carbonate

1.057: Heritage Culture and Technology

Follow-up to summer fieldwork session in Italy with Professor Admir Masic, investigating archeological sites and ancient materials

Semester-long research project on materials and sustainability: studied effects of acid rain on marble and limestone building blocks using an accelerated aging test in an acid bath

Wrote a scientific paper and presented video and 20-min presentation of findings to class and MIT Course 1 faculty members

Utilized wet saw, pH meter, scanning electron microscopy, molecular modeling software (LAMMPS, VMD), MATLAB (and videography, Adobe Premiere Rush)

Video submission to MIT CEE 2019 Video Competition

Piezoelectric Charging Insole

2.00: Introduction to Design

User experience design project, working with a local deaf college student as our user.

Created a piezoelectric shoe insole for user to be able to charge cochlear implant battery while hiking or walking.

Created and demontrated prototypes (looks-like, works-like, and proof of concept) in presentation to class and MIT Course 2 faculty.

Utilized piezoelectric elements, circuits, soldering, fabric, sewing, surveying, and user testing.
